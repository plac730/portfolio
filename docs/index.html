About Me

I am a self-taught full stack web developer based in Beaumont, CA. I enjoy creating polished, functional websites and am always looking to learn and challenge myself. As a result, I am constantly updating my knowledge and staying current with the latest trends in web development and tech in general.
I have a strong passion for coding as it not only challenges the logical side of myself, but also serves as an outlet for creativity. In order to ensure that I developed strong programming fundamentals, I completed several CS courses on data structures and object-oriented programming languages including Python, C++, and Java.
Below are some projects that demonstrate the skills I've obtained and the technologies I've worked with as a web developer. My formal education is in economics, but my interests are wide and varied. Also included is a poker game I coded in C++ combining my interests in programming and all types of strategy games.

Parks, Rec & Reviews

Live SiteGithub

Front-End: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap

Back-End: Node, Express, MongoDB, Passport, geocoder
